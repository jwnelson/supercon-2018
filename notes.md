## Badge keyboard

`kin` returns a byte entered from the keyboard.
For letters and numbers, the returned byte is the base-10 ascii encoding of the character.

Arrow keys:
* Up: 28
* down: 29
* Left: 30
* Right: 31
* space char: 32

BKSP: 127
ENTER: 10
star char: 42

Screen size: 38x19 - 0,0 in top left
